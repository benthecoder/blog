i've been craving more protein-rich breakfasts as an individual who cannot enjoy the delicacy of american breakfast like cheese and eggs. i found this specific recipe online and have been enjoying it.

## ingredients
- firm tofu
- spinach
- salmon rub powder
- sesame seeds
- smoked salmon
- turmeric powder

## steps
1. cut and pat dry tofu
2. cut into large thin pieces
3. pan fry until golden
4. start mincing it with a spatula
5. stir fry the pieces
6. once they're little pieces, add spinach
7. add salmon rub powder, sesame seeds, and turmeric powder
8. top with smoked salmon
