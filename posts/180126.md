- Pharmacy Benefit Managers (PBMs) are the reasons why drugs are expensive in the US, the [middleman](https://www.phrma.org/policy-issues/pbms-middlemen) who price drugs high.
    - 80% of drug transactions are dominated by a few major players like CVS Caremark, Express Scripts, and Optum RX
    - incentivized to favor drugs with higher list price so they can demand larger rebates from manufacturers, to increase their own earnings
    - collect billions in fees and markup drug prices, and keeping the profits rather than lowering cost
    - a product of US private insurance model. unlike SEA like Malaysia where government is the dominant payer, and are negotiating directly with manufacturers.
