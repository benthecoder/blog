ate some leftover pan fried buns i had from last night. then went to Capital One Cafe to do some work. 

at 2pm it was time to go to my dentist appointment. my dentist first told me the good news, that braces can come off. but the bad news was we can only do it on friday and i planned to leave on thursday. no big deal, i just had to move some plans around and miss my flight. talk about premature optimization. i seem to think the world revolves around me.

i packed and moved to my next hotel. in my head, i had booked different locations so that i could experience SF as much as possible before going back to Austin, so i stayed at the Marina Motel. that way i get to explore a bit of chestnut street.

after dropping off my things, i walked to peet's coffee and ordered another matcha drink, this time with pistachio milk. i booked more hotels and flights. i decided to just fly to seattle because why not?

at night i worked on a sofa and a single wooden chair, trying to eat korean food out of a plastic container.
